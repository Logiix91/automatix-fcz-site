How it works

A structured, six-phase methodology for every implementation

01

Observe

We physically visit your organization. We shadow a full process from start to finish on the work floor, run on-site interviews and working sessions with the people doing the work, and combine that with focused remote follow-up. Online surveys alone are deliberately not enough here.

02

Model

Based on what we observed, we simplify the process before any technology gets involved: which steps disappear, which get automated, and where human judgment stays necessary. This is also where we define the architecture and the success KPIs.

03

Engineer

Our team builds the working solution: AI agents, workflows and API integrations, document processing, knowledge bases and dashboards, with monitoring and logging in a controlled cloud environment from day one.

04

Integrate

All components are integrated with CRM, ERP, HR and financial systems into one infrastructure, with secure data exchange, authentication, authorization, error handling and attention to scalability and continuity.

05

Validate

Before the solution goes live, we test whether it meets the goals set in phase 2: functional, integration and performance testing, security, plus acceptance testing with your team. No measurable result means going back to the drawing board.

06

Optimize

Transformation doesn't stop at delivery. We monitor performance, availability and security, track AI quality, and continuously identify new automation opportunities.

About us

Built in Rotterdam. Built for European business.

Technology is not a goal in itself, but an instrument to help organizations operate smarter, more efficiently and in a more future-proof way.

Mission

Helping organizations work smarter by analyzing, simplifying and automating processes with AI and intelligent integrations, designed to measurably contribute to efficiency, better decision-making and higher data quality.

Vision

Over the next ten years, software shifts from a passive tool to an active participant: a digital infrastructure where people, AI and systems continuously work together. The role of the employee shifts from executing to directing.

Automatix was founded in 2026 by Ruslan and Fadil, two founders who deliberately chose to bootstrap: growing from their own revenue, with no external investors. In the early years we focus on four sectors where the combination of heavy administrative load and low AI adoption creates the fastest, most demonstrable wins, starting with logistics: the sector with the lowest AI adoption in the Netherlands, and where the founders have direct domain expertise.

We don't build off-the-shelf software. We design custom solutions that make business processes structurally more efficient, reliable and scalable, always with European data protection standards built in from the start.
